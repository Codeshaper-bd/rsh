Welcome to RSH Textile Mills Ltd, Bangladesh's leading fabric manufacturer and the country's largest exporter of local garments. Since our establishment in 2001, we have been a 100% export-oriented textile industry, dedicated to delivering high-quality products to our global customers.

At RSH Textile Mills Ltd, we take pride in our weaving unit, which enables us to ensure quick and fast delivery to meet the demands of our clients. Our commitment to global standards of quality has been the driving force behind our phenomenal growth over the last decade, allowing us to carve out a unique niche in today's competitive world market.

Our production units specialize in a range of fabric types, including pocketing fabrics such as TC (Polyester/Cotton), TR (Polyester/Rayon), and Cotton sheeting. Additionally, we offer shell fabrics such as Canvas, Twill, and Poplin, all meticulously crafted to meet the highest standards of quality and tailored to our customers' specific requirements.

With a relentless focus on delivering excellence, we have earned a reputation for producing top-notch products that meet and exceed our customers' expectations. Our commitment to quality control at every stage of production ensures that each piece of fabric leaving our facility is of the utmost quality.

As an industry leader, we understand the importance of staying ahead of trends and constantly innovating. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to keeping up with the latest market developments, enabling us to offer our customers cutting-edge solutions and unmatched service.

We believe in fostering long-term partnerships with our clients, built on trust, reliability, and exceptional performance. Whether you require fabric for garments, home textiles, or any other textile application, we are here to provide you with the highest quality products that meet your specific needs.

Explore our website to learn more about our extensive range of products and capabilities. Contact us today to experience the excellence of RSH Textile Mills Ltd and discover how we can fulfill your textile requirements with our premium fabrics and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Welcome to our company, a well-established and reputable manufacturer in the 100% export-oriented garments accessories industry in Bangladesh. We specialize in producing a wide range of accessories to complement the garment manufacturing process.

With years of experience and expertise, we have built a solid reputation as a trusted and reliable accessories manufacturing company. Our factory is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and utilizes high-quality raw materials to ensure the production of top-notch accessories.

We use various raw materials such as yarn, rubber flex, craft paper, and chemical resins to create our accessories. These materials are carefully sourced and selected to meet our stringent quality standards. By using the finest raw materials, we are able to deliver accessories that are durable, functional, and visually appealing.

Our manufacturing capacity is impressive, with the ability to produce 350,000 pieces of accessories every day. This high production output is made possible by our efficient production processes and the dedication of our skilled workforce. We take pride in our ability to meet the demands of our clients promptly and consistently.

Conveniently located in Surabari, Kashimpur, Gazipur, our factory is easily accessible for efficient logistics and timely delivery. We have a comprehensive infrastructure in place, consisting of 78 machines from different renowned brands. These machines are carefully maintained to ensure optimal performance and superior product quality.

We are committed to sustainable practices and have obtained certification under the Global Recycled Standard (GRS) Version 4.0. This certification affirms our commitment to environmentally responsible manufacturing processes and the use of recycled materials. By adhering to global standards, we contribute to a greener and more sustainable future.

At our company, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We work closely with our clients to understand their specific requirements and provide tailor-made solutions. Our dedicated team of professionals ensures that each accessory produced meets the highest quality standards and adheres to the client's specifications.

We invite you to explore our wide range of accessories and discover how we can add value to your garment manufacturing process. Contact us today to discuss your accessory needs and experience the excellence and reliability of our products and services.
